Junior Motion Designer

Home Positions Junior Motion Designer

If you have artistic ability and creative talent, you can become a graphic designer. Using your abilities and creativity, you will be able to create moving words, logos, text, etc.

Your knowledge can be used to create various media presentations such as films, advertisements, TV shows, game design.

In addition, a graphic designer is familiar with a variety of design software and computer design tools. Understanding these tools helps you stay on top of advances in the motion graphics industry.

Who is Motion designer?

The profession of motion graphics designer is a relatively new phenomenon in the field of design. Technology-based design professions are growing.

Main activities:

- creating images for the Internet, television or cinema;
- creating video clips, trailers, commercials, title sequences, etc.
- creating graphics for games

Requirements for Motion Designer:

- 3D modeling (also 2D, as a basis for 3D graphics)
- animation
- procedural animation
- texturing and lighting effects to add visual volume
- understanding the general context
- adaptability for different shapes
